When this emerging streetwear brand approached us for their Summer 2024 lookbook, they wanted something that felt authentic to Toronto's street culture. We spent weeks scouting locations—from graffiti-covered alleyways to brutalist architecture—that would serve as the perfect backdrop for their collection.

The shoot took place over a single intensive day with a lean crew and three models. Our approach was documentary-style: loose, handheld camera work that captured genuine moments alongside composed fashion shots. We wanted the clothing to feel lived-in, not posed.

The result is a visual narrative that tells the story of young creatives navigating the urban landscape. The brand loved it so much they extended our partnership for their Fall collection.
