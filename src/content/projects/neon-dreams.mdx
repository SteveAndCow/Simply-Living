Shot over three nights in downtown Toronto, *Neon Dreams* captures the electric energy of the city after dark. We worked closely with the artist to develop a visual language that honored the retro-wave aesthetic while pushing it into contemporary territory.

The project presented unique challenges—shooting in public spaces with limited time windows, managing practical neon lighting, and coordinating with a dozen background performers. Our approach centered on fluid camera movement and bold color grading to create a dreamlike atmosphere that complements the track's hypnotic rhythm.

This piece marked a turning point for Simply Living Studios, showcasing our ability to blend technical precision with artistic vision on a modest budget.
